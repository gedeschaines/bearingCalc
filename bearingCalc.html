Carolina Bay Inferred Orientation Calculator

The calculator takes two input classes:
  1. A single Google Earth "Placemark" element. Generates a predicted inferred orientation.
  2. A user-oriented "Bearing Arrow" overlay element. Generates a de-Skewed walk-back to crater.
